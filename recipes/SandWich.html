SAND WICH

Description

Sand Wich

it comes to life with a selection of fresh vegetables. Crisp lettuce leaves, juicy sliced tomatoes, crunchy cucumbers, vibrant bell peppers, and thinly sliced onions add texture and a burst of flavors. You can customize your veg sandwich with your favorite vegetables or use a mix of colorful options for visual appeal.



INGRIDIENTS

  • Bread
  • Spreads and Sauces
  • Salt
  • Potatoes
  • vegetables and greens
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Gather your ingredients:

    • Bread slices (2 slices per sandwich)
    • Spread or sauce of your choice (mayonnaise, mustard, etc.).
    • Vegetables (lettuce, tomatoes, cucumbers, onions, etc.).
    • Cheese (optional)
    • Protein (tofu, tempeh, chickpea salad, etc.) - optional
    • Additional toppings (olives, pickles, etc.) - optional

  2. Prepare the ingredients:

    • Wash and slice the vegetables.
    • If using protein, ensure it is cooked and ready to use.

  3. Layer the fillings:

    • Start with a layer of vegetables, such as lettuce, on one slice of bread.
    • Add the sliced tomatoes, cucumbers, onions, and any other desired vegetables.
    • f using cheese, place it on top of the vegetables.

  4. Optional: Toast or grill the sandwich:

    • If desired, you can toast the sandwich in a sandwich press or grill it in a pan with a little butter or oil for a warm, crispy texture. This step is optional and depends on your preference..

  5. When the Sandwich is baked properly servet the Sandwich and enjoy!
